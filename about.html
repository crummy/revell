Vincent Revell - Bachelor of Engineering (Hons)

Vincent has a keen interest in sustainable urban development and emerging trends in residential housing. With over ten years of experience in contract and project management both in New Zealand and in Germany, Vincent has worked on multimillion-dollar projects with high levels of complexity. As a highly adaptable individual, Vincent integrates quickly into collaborative team environments, where he injects energy and a sense of innovative thinking. Vincent actively seeks out opportunities to develop people and bring out the best in them. 


Core Competencies:

  • Navigating Resource and Building Consents
  • Consultant and Contractor Procurement
  • Design Coordination
  • Programme Management
  • Establishing a Project Budget and Financial Tracking
  • Risk Identification and Mitigation
  • Stakeholder Management
  • Providing clarity where there is ambiguity
