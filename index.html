Revell Developments provides project management services. Not unlike the conductor of an orchestra, we coordinate all the elements of a project to ensure a successful outcome is achieved.


We make it our business to understand each project role (architect, planner, engineer, etc.) and are accountable for tracking the deliverables of each role against the project budget and timeframes. No two projects are the same. We adapt to the unique requirements of each project so that we can provide sound advice to clients on strategy, risks and options. This enables clients to make decisions and feel in control of their project. 


Community Housing

Multi-unit housing developments are not without their complexities. We undertake:


  • Master planning including infrastructure and staging management.
  • Cashflow forecasting.
  • Resource Consent, Building Consent and Subdivision processing.
  • Consultant and Contractor management.
  • Management of investment and financing.

High-end Residential and Luxury Accommodation

Quality design and attention to detail are paramount in this space. We:

﻿

  • Identify the right consultants and build the team necessary to deliver the project.
  • Work with cost consultants to establish a project budget.
  • Produce a clear design brief that reflects the outcomes the client wants.
  • Establish a project programme to track against.
  • Identify and mitigate key project risks.

Urban Regeneration and Infrastructure

New Zealand is waking up to the idea that our towns and cities need to develop differently going forward, so that the urban environment is vibrant and livable. We:


  • Understand the principles of good urban design.
  • Provide stakeholder and community facilitation.
  • Persevere despite deep-seated mindsets that oppose change.
  • Think creatively and find solutions.
  • Pay attention to detail.
